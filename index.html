About Us

Studio Ghibli is a world-renowned Japanese animation film studio celebrated for its imaginative storytelling, emotional depth, and breathtaking hand-drawn artistry. Founded in 1985, the studio has created timeless films that captivate audiences of all ages across the globe.


At Studio Ghibli, we believe that stories have the power to inspire, heal, and transform. Our films explore themes of courage, friendship, love, family, and the deep connection between humanity and nature. Through beautifully crafted characters and magical worlds, we strive to create meaningful experiences that stay in the hearts of viewers long after the credits roll.

Mission

To produce heartfelt movies with rich storytelling, meaningful characters, and hand-drawn artistry that honor emotion, creativity, and cultural depth-bringing wonder and warmth to every generation.

Vision

To create timeless animated stories that celebrate imagination, humanity, and the beauty of nature, inspiring audiences of all ages around the world.

Our Portofolio

Arrietty

When a tiny "borrower" named Arrietty makes friends with a young boy many times her size, she must also shield her family from his towering elders.

Grave of the Fireflies

Two orphans fight for survival in post-World War II Japan, but society is harsh and they struggle with finding food and escaping the hardships of war.

Howl's Moving Castle

Teenager Sophie works in her late father's hat shop in a humdrum town, but things get interesting when she's transformed into an elderly woman.

Spirited Away

A 10-year-old girl named Chihiro wanders into a mysterious world where a witch rules — and those who disobey her are turned into animals.

My Neighbor Totoro

While spending a summer in the Japanese countryside with their father, two young sisters befriend mystical creatures who live in the nearby forest.

The Wind Rises

A decades-spanning tale inspired by the life of Jirô Horikoshi, designer of the World War II fighter plane the Zero.
